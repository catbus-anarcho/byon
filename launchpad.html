How it works

Connect your wallet

Use a Bitcoin Runes wallet like Xverse to connect. Xverse lets you store and send ordinals securely and even works with hardware wallets like Ledger.

Submit a collection

Members can propose new ordinal collections through a simple form. No minting fee is charged – just your creativity on display.

Vote & Launch

Submitted collections are displayed publicly. Community members vote on proposals to decide which collections go live.

Bring your node

When a collection is approved, the creator can connect their own Bitcoin node running ord client. BYON ensures control and decentralisation—users never relinquish custody.

Connect your wallet

To participate in BYON you must prove membership by connecting a compatible wallet. We currently support Xverse. Once connected, a whitelist check will grant access.
